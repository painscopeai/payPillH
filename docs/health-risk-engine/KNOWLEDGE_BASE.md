# Knowledge base (dashboard wellness)

## Purpose

PayPill shows **wellness-style summaries** from information you save (onboarding, profile, vitals). These are **not diagnoses** and **not** substitutes for NHS risk calculators or clinician judgement.

## UK context

General prevention and cardiovascular health are discussed in **NICE NG238** and related guidance. PayPill does **not** replicate statutory QOF or NHS risk-assessment outputs.

## Product stance

- Prefer clear language (“wellness score”, “burden index”) over algorithm names.  
- Always show an **informational disclaimer** on the dashboard.  
- For software that could influence clinical decisions, follow **MHRA** / medical-device rules separately from this repo’s heuristic layer.
